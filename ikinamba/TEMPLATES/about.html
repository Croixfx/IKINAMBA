Background of study

In Rwanda vehicle owners often face the challenge of maintaining clean cars amidst busy schedules and limited access to efficient car wash services

Recognizing this gap, Smartshine Car Wash was born out of a vision to revolutionize vehicle care by introducing a seamless, technology-driven experience.

The idea was sparked during a brainstorming session on integrating AI, IoT, and web-based solutions to simplify everyday services. The founder, deeply passionate about technology and innovation, envisioned a world where car owners could effortlessly book car wash services, track their appointments, and choose between manual or automated cleaning — all with a few taps on their mobile device.

Smartshine Car Wash not only aims to save time and enhance convenience but also to support sustainability by promoting eco-friendly cleaning methods. By offering membership models for both individuals and corporate clients, Smartshine ensures flexibility and affordability.

What started as a simple idea has evolved into a commitment to providing Rwanda’s first AI-powered car wash service, transforming car care into a hassle-free, modern experience. With innovation at its core, Smartshine is on a mission to redefine how car owners maintain their vehicles.

Belief root source of idea

The core belief that inspired Smartshine Car Wash is grounded in the idea that technology should simplify everyday tasks and improve quality of life. This belief stems from the understanding that:

In today’s fast-paced world, people value efficiency. Waiting in long lines at traditional car washes or struggling to find quality service shouldn't be the norm. Technology can help reduce these inefficiencies.

With the rise of smartphones, AI, and IoT, services should be as accessible as a tap on a screen. By leveraging these technologies, Smartshine believes car wash services can be smarter, faster, and more user-friendly.
